Simple way of knowing your Akan name.

In Ghananian culture you can know your Akan(names given as per the day of the week that you were born), by simply knowing your birthday.

In this web site , i have made things easier for you.

Just put your date of birth in this format(YY-MM-DD),then enter your gender(female or male) and your name will be displayed automatically.
